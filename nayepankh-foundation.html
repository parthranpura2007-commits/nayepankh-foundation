What We Do

Programs That Transform Lives

From street classrooms to menstrual hygiene drives — each initiative is hands-on, volunteer-run, and built for real, lasting impact.

📚

Pathshala — Mobile Education

Street-side tutoring units staffed by student volunteers, delivering basic literacy, stationery, and textbooks to children of daily-wage workers.

🍱

Hunger Relief

Hot nutritional meals distributed daily to pavement dwellers, along with warm winter blankets and clothing during the cold months.

🌸

Menstrual Hygiene

Biodegradable sanitary napkins distributed directly to women in remote settlements, paired with awareness campaigns on personal hygiene.

🐾

Animal Welfare

Extending compassion beyond humans — feeding stray animals and supporting the welfare of community animals across our operating cities.

🏥

Health & Hygiene Kits

Personal care products delivered directly to women and children in underserved communities who lack access to basic healthcare supplies.

🤝

Community Internships

Structured volunteer internships for students in education, HR, tech, and social impact — building the next generation of changemakers.

Our Story

Youth-Led. Community-Rooted. Impact-Driven.

NayePankh was born during India's toughest lockdown — when a group of young people decided that waiting wasn't an option. What began as emergency relief quickly grew into one of the country's largest student-led organizations.

Operating in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and beyond, our volunteers — many still in school or college — show up every day to serve communities that have been left behind. We believe service to humankind is service to the divine.

Our name says it all: Naye Pankh — new wings. We exist to help people rise.
